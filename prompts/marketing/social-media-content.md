# Social Media Content

## Purpose
Generate short-form social media posts around a specific topic with platform limits and hashtags.

## Best For
- Twitter/X content
- campaign ideation
- awareness posts

## Recommended Model
ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini

## Inputs
- topic
- platform
- number of posts
- tone
- hashtag direction

## Prompt
Create five engaging Twitter posts (280 characters max) about the importance of sustainable living.

Requirements:
- Each post must be 280 characters or less
- Make them engaging and easy to understand
- Include relevant hashtags
- Vary the angle slightly across the five posts
- Keep the tone positive and motivating

## Example Input
- Topic: sustainable living
- Platform: Twitter/X
- Number of posts: 5
- Tone: engaging and practical

## Example Output
Five short posts with hooks, concise messaging, and hashtags related to sustainability.

## Notes
- Strong for batch social content generation
- You can improve output by specifying audience, brand voice, or campaign goal
