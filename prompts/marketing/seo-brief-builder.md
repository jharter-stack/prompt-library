# SEO Brief Builder

## Purpose
Generate a structured SEO content brief for a target keyword, audience, and business goal.

## Best For
- blog planning
- content strategy
- service page planning

## Recommended Model
ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini

## Inputs
- target keyword
- target audience
- business or brand
- product or service
- tone
- content goal

## Prompt
You are an SEO content strategist.

Create a detailed SEO content brief using the information below.

Inputs:
- Target keyword: [INSERT]
- Target audience: [INSERT]
- Business or brand: [INSERT]
- Product or service: [INSERT]
- Tone: [INSERT]
- Content goal: [INSERT]

Your job is to produce:
1. A working title
2. Search intent
3. Primary audience pain points
4. Key questions the content should answer
5. Suggested H1
6. Suggested H2 and H3 structure
7. Important points to include
8. Suggested call to action
9. Internal content ideas to link to
10. A short writing brief for the writer

Keep the output practical, clear, and ready for a writer to use immediately.

## Example Input
- Target keyword: AI prompt library
- Target audience: small business owners
- Business or brand: MTS Prompts
- Product or service: reusable prompt systems
- Tone: clear and professional
- Content goal: attract organic traffic and explain value

## Example Output
- Working title: What an AI Prompt Library Is and How Small Businesses Can Use One
- Search intent: informational / commercial
- Pain points: wasted time, inconsistent AI results, lack of process
- CTA: explore the MTS Prompts library

## Notes
- Works best when the keyword is specific
- Add competitor URLs separately if you want a deeper content brief
- Strong for top-of-funnel and middle-of-funnel content
