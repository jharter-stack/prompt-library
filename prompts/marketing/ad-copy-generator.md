# Ad Copy Generator

## Purpose
Generate ad copy variations for a product, service, or offer.

## Best For
- paid ads
- social promotions
- landing page headline ideas

## Recommended Model
ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini

## Inputs
- brand
- product or service
- audience
- offer
- tone
- platform

## Prompt
You are a direct-response copywriter.

Create ad copy variations using the information below.

Inputs:
- Brand: [INSERT]
- Product or service: [INSERT]
- Audience: [INSERT]
- Offer: [INSERT]
- Tone: [INSERT]
- Platform: [Facebook / Instagram / Google / LinkedIn / X / other]

Produce:
1. 10 headline options
2. 5 primary text variations
3. 5 call-to-action lines
4. 3 angle variations based on different motivations
5. One short version and one stronger, more direct version

Requirements:
- Focus on clarity and audience pain points
- Avoid generic hype
- Make the copy sound usable in the real world
- Match the platform where possible

## Example Input
- Brand: MTS Prompts
- Product or service: reusable AI prompt systems
- Audience: creators and small business owners
- Offer: starter prompt library
- Tone: confident and modern
- Platform: Instagram

## Example Output
- Headlines focused on speed, clarity, and better AI output
- Primary text variations for audience pain points
- CTA lines such as Explore the library or Start using smarter prompts

## Notes
- Good for rough ideation and first-pass copy
- Best when the audience and offer are specific
- You can ask for pain-point, benefit-first, or curiosity-driven angles
